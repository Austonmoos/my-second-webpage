Aitana's Dogs - Argus & Zagreus

Argus and Zagreus, or Gus and Zag for short, are both silken windhounds. They love to zoom really fast for a few minutes, and then lay flat on the floor for the rest of the day. Their favorite treats are bully sticks and chicken jerky.
